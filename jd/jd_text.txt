- Build and deploy machine learning models for real-world problems.
- Collaborate with data scientists, engineers, and product managers.

- Strong understanding of algorithms and statistics.
- Experience in deploying ML models using Flask or Streamlit.
- Good communication skills and problem-solving mindset.
